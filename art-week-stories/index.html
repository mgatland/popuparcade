The Pop-Up Games Arcade presents:

Choose Our Own Adventure: A story-making game

PITT STREET METHODIST CHURCH

  • Story Making 1: Wed 15th October, 7pm to 9pm
  • Story Making 2: Thurs 16th October, 7pm to 9pm
  • Story Arcade: Sat 18th October, 2pm to 4:30pm

Come along on Wednesday or Thursday night to take part in a story-making game! We'll work as a group to make an interactive story, where the reader can make choices to as they go. You don't need to book or bring anything. Just turn up!

Children are welcome but must be accompanied by an adult.

Come on Saturday to play the finished stories in digital form, running on Pop-Up Arcade's hand-painted computers.
